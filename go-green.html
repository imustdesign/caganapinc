"GO GREEN"

We at Caganap, Inc. believe in making the largest impact with the smallest carbon footprint. We have access to eco-friendly materials and have the right processes to give you a truly green product. What's the use of buying eco-friendly wood if its sprayed with a high VOC lacquer? We recognize that truly "going green" is a combinatino of materials and manufacturing processes.

All of our products meet the California Air Research Board (CARB) specifications. All of our raw wood materials are manufactures with less formaldehyde which means less chemicals in the production of composite wood. We are also in the process of switching to a completely water based paint operation which will greatly exceed the standards we currently meet, set by AQMD (Air Quality Management District).

It is important to safeguard the health of the environment for future enjoyment and preservation. We at Caganap, Inc. are doing everything that we can to use less, use smarter, and use more eco-friendly materials. Whether it's our commitment to our company wide recycling program or using materials that exceed federal and state environmental requirements, we remain conscious of protecting and conserving the Earth.
