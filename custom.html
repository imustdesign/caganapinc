CUSTOM

"If you can think it, we can build it." We workin a variety of media including wood, metal, glass, plastic, fabric, resin. We offer the ability to integrate these elements into visually appealing products that capture your vision and fit your needs.

Our team has over 50 years of store planning, visual merchandising and engineering experience. Our creative staff will work with you to design and develop your fixtures and value engineer each design to translate it into a functional reality.

We offer the service of a "one stop shopping" program, enabling you, our client, to plan all the elements of your project without the need to coordinate the efforts of serveral different vendors. We will help design, fabricate, pack, ship and install fixtures in your locations. From initial concept to installation, we will be with you every step of the way, translating your needs and directions into 3-dimensional realities.
