ABOUT CAGANAP, INC.

Caganap Inc. is a family-owned design and custom display manufacturing company with over fifty years of experience in the retail, visual merchandising and hospitality industries. Established in the 1960's, our organization is a fully equipped design and production facility for the manufacture and distribution of both hard and soft goods. We specialize in high-end furniture and fixtures, point of purchase displays, promotional and visual props and trade show booths.

Initially located in Los Angeles, Caganap Inc twice outgrew its facilities and eventually found a home in Montebelle, CA. In 2006, STC Display, Inc purchased Caganap Inc and moved the facility to its current location in the City of Commerce, just outside Los Angeles.
